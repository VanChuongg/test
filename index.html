Cristiano Ronaldo

About

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro is a Portuguese professional footballer widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time. Known for his incredible goal-scoring ability, athleticism, and leadership on and off the field.

Quick Facts

Personal Info

  • Full name: Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro
  • Born: February 5, 1985 (Funchal, Madeira, Portugal)
  • Height: 1.87 m
  • Position: Forward
  • Current club: Al Nassr (Saudi Arabia)

Achievements

  1. 5× Ballon d'Or winner
  2. UEFA European Champion (2016)
  3. 5× UEFA Champions League titles
  4. All-time top scorer in UEFA Champions League
  5. Over 850 career goals
